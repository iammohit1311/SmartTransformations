Internet of Things Training

How It Works

IOT

Internet of Things combines strengths and solutions from several industries to provide new capabilities and solutions to individuals, businesses and industries.

Workshop

Two-day workshop on the Internet of Things.

Benefits to the participants

The participants after completion will have a great understanding of IoT solution architecture, deployment framework, IoT ecosystem

Who Should Attend

Mid-level to senior business professionals such as general manager, vice president, department head, division head, project leader, program leader.

Technology leaders from the industry who wish to take advantage of the IoT related technologies and businesses.

Members of the faculty from universities and colleges.

Executives from the following industries

  • Electronics, devices, sensors, telecom
  • Consulting firms
  • Information technology and management
  • Civil infrastructure
  • Project management, governance support
  • Construction

Government senior-level officials

  • Involved in strategy, conceptualization, prioritization
  • Governing, managing projects
  • A part of the leadership team of a Smart city initiative of the government
  • City planning, architecture
  • Contractor management and governance

Consultants who advise on

  • Planning, execution, operations of IoT deployments
  • Governance, stakeholder engagement

Benefits to the Participants

The participants after completion will have a great understanding of

  • The business opportunities in the IoT space for the business they are in.
  • The needed definitions, concepts, strategies, approaches, and technologies to develop and manage an IoT-driven deployment or transformation
  • The business framework, key industry participants, business models
  • IoT solution architecture, deployment framework, IoT ecosystem
